Notes:
The National Library of Norway's archive in Mo i Rana is a secure, high-tech storage facility in mountain halls that houses and digitises Norway's cultural heritage, but it is not open to the public and requires special permission for visits. While the Mo i Rana location handles the physical and digital preservation, the public can visit the National Library's public-facing building in Oslo, which has exhibitions, reading rooms, and events.
