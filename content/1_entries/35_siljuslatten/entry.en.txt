Notes: Siljuslåtten nursing home is located in the Ytrebygda district by the Råstøl light railstop andhas 90 residents divided into five departments. The hospital opened on 1 April 2019 and is operated by the Diakonova Haraldsplass Foundation with the goal of providing a good home for the elderly and care-dependent in Bergen. The nursing home has great facilities, including atrial gardens and a roof terrace. Donated stones come from the outdoor areas.
