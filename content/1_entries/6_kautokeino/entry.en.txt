Čoarvemátta is a new building that brings together theSámi National Theatre, Beaivváš, which presents stories and theatrical experiences based on Sámi cultural practices and worldviews, with the Sámi High School and Reindeer Herding School, which teaches traditional knowledge, culture and language to Sámi youth.
