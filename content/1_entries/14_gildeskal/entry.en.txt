Notes:
The quartzite quarry at Mårnes is operated by Elkem ASA, a Chinese-owned, Norwegian industrial group with origins back to 1904. Elkem produces silicon and silicon products and covers the entire value chain from quartz to specialised silicon products, ferrosilicon and carbon materials. The donated stone is left over from the quarry production.
