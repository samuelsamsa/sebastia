Surplus from carving a public sculpture in sandstone, Geological monument by Kristian Blystad.
The Norwegian Oil Museum is a museum that documents the history of the petroleum industry off the Norwegian coast from its beginnings in the 1960s. The museum is located in Stavanger and has a large collection of technical equipment from the pioneer era onwards. The museum building, Kjerringholmen, was designed by architects Lunde & Løvseth.
