Notes:
Two stones have been donated from Giljagarden in Gjesdal. One stone was taken from a wall in a stone house, and one stone is an older fence post.
The farm is a private smallholding, where the owners also organise art and photo exhibitions, children's theatre and various literary events in the sheep house.
