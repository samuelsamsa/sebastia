Notes:
Stone from the construction of the power station approx. 1905.
Rectangular granite block that is available on the shore along the quay at the protected Tysso I power station. The block has probably been left as waste from the construction process. It’s clear from the marks on the stone that it has been carved out; this was done by hand, with hammer and chisel.
