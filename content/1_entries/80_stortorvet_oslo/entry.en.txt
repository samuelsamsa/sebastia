Notes:
The stone donation comes from the sculptureWater in Stone. It is made of red granite as a largebasin andis located at Stortorvet in Oslo. The sculpture was made by Anne-Marie Backer Mohr (1928-2005) and Bjart Mohr (1925-2023), who won a national ornamentation competition held in 1985. The sculpture was paid for by collected funds and grants from the Oslo municipality, and it was inaugurated in 1993.
Stortorvet has been a central square since1736 andis an important part of the public space in Oslo.
