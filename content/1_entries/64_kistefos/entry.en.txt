Notes:
This is one of the stones "scrapped" after use. We can't extract just any one of them from the park, so we had to see and look for a nice piece that was possible to get out. The stone is part of a grindstone. This is most likely a natural stone, which was used in the beginning, before the reinforced stone took over. It is covered in some moss after being in a pile of stones.
(link: https://www.kistefosmuseum.com/industry/the-history-of-kistefos text: Kistefos Museum)
