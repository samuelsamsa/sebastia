Trefoldighetskirken is a neo-Gothic cruciform church in Oslo, and is the parish church of Trefoldighet congregation. The church was built in 1850-1858. In 1956-58, the church was restored and returned to its original appearance.
The church is located on Akersgata at the foot of Hammersborghøyden.
Oslo's rapid growth in the mid-19th century had made the old cathedral too small, and the new church was built on the outskirts of the then urban area.
