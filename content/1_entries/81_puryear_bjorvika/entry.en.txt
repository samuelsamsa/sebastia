Notes:
The stones are remnants from the production of the sculptureCreature from Iddefjordby Martin Puryear, which stands outside the Norwegian Main Library, Deichman Bjørvika in Oslo. It was installed in 2020 and is part of the Sparebankstiftelsen DnB project "Skulpturstopp", which finances public artworks in Eastern Norway.
