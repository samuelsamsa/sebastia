Notes:
The stones is from light rail stops from phase 1 of the development.
“White line - is the marker in the urban landscape - the hallmark of our line, and an important part of the Light Rail brand. It helps to create an aesthetic, tidy and urban expression on the line route, where it passes through various landscape and urban spaces”.
Evjen granite is used at all stops (with a few exceptions), and has been included since the design competition (2005) ahead of construction phase 1; “Holdeplass (rail stop) is the Light Rail’s handshake to the traveller. It is safe, dry and accessible to everyone. Furnishings, glass walls and real-time information all provide a space for relaxed waiting and the certainty that you will see when the train arrives.
