Notes:
The stones from the cathedral were taken from under the floor of the cathedral during the last restoration completed in 2025. They were probably placed under the floor after the city fire in 1272, when the cathedral was damaged. They have old mortar on them and were likely part of a choir wall, which was changed with the construction of the cathedral.
