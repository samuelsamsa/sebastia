Notes:
The stones were originally part of the "German order" during the years of occupation, 1941-45.
The stones were to be used in Hitler's construction of Germania.
(link: https://usercontent.one/wp/www.osloelveforum.org/wp-content/uploads/2023/06/20221120-Den-tyske-bestilling.pdf text: 20221120-Den-tyske-bestilling.pdf (usercontent.one))
Some of these stones were used in the sculpture park at Klosterenga by Bård Breivik (1948-2016) and were completed by Kristian Blystad (1946).
